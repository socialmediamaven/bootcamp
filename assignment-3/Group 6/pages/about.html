The East Palo Alto Senior Center was established in 1982 for those who are age 55 or over or spouse of a Senior.

As a multipurpose Senior Center, it offers a variety of activities and assistance to Seniors.

The staff and volunteers provide information about resources in such areas as housing, employment, household help, recreation, financial assistance, health services and in-home help. Health education and screenings are provided at the East Palo Alto Senior Center.

Continental breakfast and hot lunch meals are served during the week, Monday through Friday. A brown bag program is offered every Thursday.

Transportation is available to take Seniors to doctor appointments and on essential errands.
