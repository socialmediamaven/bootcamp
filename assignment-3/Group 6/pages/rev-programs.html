Transportation

Palo Alto Shuttle Service

The City of Palo Alto's Shuttle is free and open to everyone.

All shuttles are wheelchair accessible but wheelchairs must have a working brake system.

Shuttle stops are marked with a "Palo Alto Shuttle" sign,

a sticker on a regular VTA bus stop sign,

or a Shuttle decal on a stop sign pole in residential areas

you can view the shuttle schedule online in

an interactive map by clicking this link.
